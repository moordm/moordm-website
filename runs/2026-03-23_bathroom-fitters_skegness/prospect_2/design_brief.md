# Design Brief: Webb & Sons — Landing Page

## StoryBrand Framework

### 1. Hero Section
- **Headline:** "Family Bathroom Fitters Serving Skegness and Lincolnshire"
- **Subheadline:** "Kitchens and bathrooms designed, supplied, and fitted by a family team you can trust"
- **CTA Button:** "Get a Free Quote"
- **Phone number:** 07702 029225 (prominently displayed)
- **Hero image:** Pexels luxury bathroom with freestanding tub (hero_image)

### 2. Problem Section
- **Customer frustration:** Finding a bathroom fitter you can actually rely on feels impossible. You want someone who'll turn up when they say they will, do a proper job, and not charge the earth. You need a team that handles everything — plumbing, tiling, the lot — without you having to chase three different tradespeople.

### 3. Solution / Services Section
- **Full Bathroom Installations** — Complete design-to-installation service
- **Kitchen Fitting** — Bespoke kitchen supply and fit
- **Plumbing Services** — All pipework, drainage, and connections handled in-house
- **Repairs & Updates** — Smaller jobs too: new taps, radiators, shower replacements
- **Secondary image** used here showing marble-tiled bathroom

### 4. Social Proof Section
- "A trusted family business — the name Webb & Sons means quality workmanship passed down through generations"
- Plumbing expertise: "With qualified plumbers on the team, every installation is watertight — literally"

### 5. Process Section
1. **Give Us a Ring** — Tell us what you're after, and we'll arrange a free visit
2. **We Design & Quote** — We measure up and give you a clear, honest quote
3. **We Fit It Right** — Our family team handles everything, start to finish

### 6. CTA / Enquiry Section
- Enquiry form: name, phone, email, message
- "Prefer to talk? Call us: 07702 029225"

### 7. Footer

## Visual Design

### Colour Palette
- **Primary:** Navy blue (#1B3A5C) — trustworthy, established, family business feel
- **Accent:** Soft gold (#C5A55A) — warmth, quality, premium
- **Background:** Clean white (#FFFFFF) with light grey sections (#F5F5F5)
- **Text:** Near-black (#111111)

### Typography
- **Display:** "Playfair Display" — classic, established, suits a family business brand
- **Body:** "Lato" — warm, friendly, readable

### Tone
- Warm, family-oriented, honest. A business that's been doing this for years and does it properly. No jargon, no hard sell — just straightforward quality.
