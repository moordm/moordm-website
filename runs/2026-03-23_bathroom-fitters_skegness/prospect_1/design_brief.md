## Visual Design

### Colour Palette
- **Primary:** Deep slate (#2C3E50) — professional, trustworthy
- **Accent:** Warm copper/bronze (#B87333) — premium craft feel, suits joinery trade
- **Background:** Warm off-white (#FAF8F5)
- **Text:** Dark charcoal (#1A1A1A)

### Typography
- **Display:** "DM Serif Display" — elegant, editorial, stands out from generic trade sites
- **Body:** "Inter" — wait, the skill says no Inter. Use "Source Sans 3" — clean, modern, highly readable

### Tone
- Friendly, confident, local. Like chatting to a mate who happens to be brilliant at what he does. Not salesy — let the reviews speak for themselves.
