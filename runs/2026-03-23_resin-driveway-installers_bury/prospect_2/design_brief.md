## Colour Palette
- Primary: #2D5016 (deep forest green — trades/professional)
- Secondary: #C8A96E (warm sand/stone)
- Accent: #FAFAF5 (warm cream)
- Text: #2C2C2C (near-black)

## Typography
- Display: "Playfair Display" (Google Fonts) — classic, premium
- Body: "Nunito Sans" — friendly and readable

## Tone
- Friendly and approachable — local tradesperson
- Trustworthy — perfect reviews, registered installer
- Practical — clear pricing, no jargon
