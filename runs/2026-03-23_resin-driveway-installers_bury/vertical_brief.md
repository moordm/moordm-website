# Vertical Brief: Resin Driveway Installers — Bury, Greater Manchester

## Date: 2026-03-23

## Competitors Analysed
1. **Driveways of Bury** (drivewaysofbury.co.uk) — 5.0★ (11 reviews), family run, 20+ years
2. **Aurora Resin Driveways** (auroraresin.co.uk) — 5.0★ (62 reviews), resin specialists
3. **Classic Stone Driveways** (classicstonedriveways.co.uk) — Award-winning, approved installer

## What the Best Sites Always Include
- Hero section with a strong, specific headline about location (Bury/Manchester) and service
- High-quality photo of a completed resin driveway (front of house, lifestyle shot)
- Immediate trust signals: years of experience, guarantee length, free quotes
- Phone number prominently displayed in header/hero
- Lead capture form visible above the fold
- Google reviews or testimonials with real names and star ratings
- Services listed with individual pages (resin bound, resin bonded, block paving, patios)
- Gallery/portfolio of completed work
- "Areas We Cover" section for local SEO
- Clear 3-step process section (enquire → site visit → installation)

## Trust Signals That Appear Most
- Years of experience (20-25 years common)
- 10-year guarantee on all work
- Free no-obligation quotes / free site surveys
- "Family run business" messaging
- Google review count and star rating displayed
- Approved/registered installer badges (Resiblock etc)
- "Best quality materials" commitment
- Before/after photos of actual projects

## What Calls to Action Convert
- "Get a Free Quote" / "Get a Free Estimate" (primary)
- "Click to Call Now" (mobile-first)
- Phone number as clickable button in header
- Contact form with minimal fields (name, phone, email, postcode, message)
- WhatsApp link (Aurora uses this effectively)

## Visual Tone
- **Professional/trade** with aspirational lifestyle imagery
- Hero images: completed driveways in front of attractive houses, ideally with car parked
- Colour palettes in the sector:
  - Dark navy/charcoal + orange/amber (Driveways of Bury)
  - Teal/dark green + white (Aurora Resin, Classic Stone)
  - Green + gold/cream also works well
- Clean, modern fonts — not overly decorative
- Plenty of whitespace between sections
- Before/after imagery powerful for conversion

## Recommended Section Structure for Our Builds
1. **Hero** — Bold headline, sub-headline with location, CTA button, phone number
2. **Trust bar** — Years experience, guarantee, free quotes, review count
3. **Services** — 3-4 core services with icons and brief descriptions
4. **About / Why Us** — Short story, family business angle if applicable, local credibility
5. **Gallery** — 3-4 project photos showing quality of work
6. **Testimonials** — 2-3 real Google reviews with names and star ratings
7. **Process** — Simple 3-step: Call → Site Visit → Installation
8. **Contact/CTA** — Form with phone, email, postcode fields + phone number + map
9. **Footer** — Business name, address, phone, areas covered
