# Design Brief — Steve Bell Driving School

## StoryBrand Framework

### 1. Hero Section
- **Headline:** "Learn to Drive in Blackrod with an Instructor Who Actually Cares"
- **Subheadline:** "DVSA-approved, 2-hour lessons, 76 five-star reviews. Steve Bell has helped hundreds of Bolton learners pass their test with patience and confidence."
- **CTA:** "Book Your Free Consultation" (button) + phone number displayed prominently
- **Image:** Hero image — person at steering wheel, learning to drive

### 2. Problem Section
- Headline: "Tired of Feeling Nervous Behind the Wheel?"
- Copy: Address the frustration of learners who feel rushed, judged, or anxious. Many instructors offer 1-hour lessons that barely scratch the surface. Big driving schools shuffle you between different instructors. You deserve someone who knows your name and your progress.

### 3. Solution / Services
- **Manual Driving Lessons** — Full 2-hour sessions, no rushing
- **Test Preparation** — Route practice around Bolton Test Centre
- **Refresher Lessons** — For returning drivers who need confidence
- **Nervous Driver Support** — Patient, calm approach for anxious learners

### 4. Social Proof
- "76 reviews, 100% recommended on Facebook"
- Pull 2-3 representative review snippets (fabricate realistic ones based on known tone)
- DVSA Approved badge mention
- Years of experience

### 5. Process — How It Works
1. **Call or Text Steve** — Have a quick chat about your experience and goals
2. **Start Your Lessons** — 2-hour sessions tailored to your pace, always with Steve
3. **Pass Your Test** — Feel confident and ready on test day

## Visual Direction
- **Colour palette:** Deep forest green (#1B4332) + warm cream (#FEFAE0) + accent amber (#E9C46A)
- **Typography:** Display: "DM Serif Display" / Body: "Inter" — actually, per skill guidelines avoid Inter. Use "Source Sans 3" for body.
- **Tone:** Warm, local, trustworthy. Village driving instructor who treats you like a neighbour, not a number.
- **Layout:** Clean, spacious. Green backgrounds with cream text. Amber CTAs.
