# Vertical Design Brief — Driving Instructors, Bolton

## Competitor Analysis Summary

**Competitors analysed:**
1. Darz Driving School (darz-ds.co.uk) — Bolton's largest, 45 instructors, 4.9★ Trustpilot (887 reviews)
2. Shah Driving School (shahdrivingschool.uk) — Professional, multilingual instructors
3. Harwood Driving School (harwooddrivingschool.com) — DVSA ORDIT trainer, premium positioning

## What the Best Sites Always Include
- Clear headline focused on passing your test (customer outcome, not business ego)
- Lesson types: manual, automatic, intensive/crash courses
- Pricing transparency (per hour or package deals)
- Areas covered — specific Bolton postcodes and surrounding towns
- DVSA approval badge or mention
- Pass rate statistics where available
- Direct phone number prominently displayed
- Online booking or enquiry form

## Trust Signals That Appear Most
- DVSA approved instructor badge
- Google/Trustpilot review count and star rating
- Years of experience (Harwood emphasises ORDIT Grade 1 status)
- Pass rate percentage (Darz claims 80%+ manual pass rate)
- Number of students passed
- "100% recommend" from Facebook
- Free theory support / online resources

## Calls to Action That Convert
- "Book Your First Lesson" (primary)
- "Call/Text Now" with phone number
- "Get a Free Quote"
- Simple enquiry form: name, phone, email, preferred lesson type

## Visual Tone
- Professional but approachable — not corporate
- Car/road imagery — real driving situations
- Bold, confident colours (Darz uses dark navy + yellow; Harwood uses dark green + white)
- Clean modern layouts, not cluttered
- Mobile-first — most learners are young and browse on phone

## Recommended Section Structure
1. Hero — Headline + subheadline + CTA + phone number
2. Trust bar — Key stats (years, pass rate, reviews, DVSA approved)
3. Services — Lesson types with brief descriptions (manual, auto, intensive)
4. How It Works — 3-step process
5. Testimonials / Reviews — Real quotes with names
6. About the Instructor — Personal, local angle
7. Areas Covered — Bolton and surrounding postcodes
8. Contact / Enquiry Form — Name, phone, email, message
9. Footer — Phone, email, address, business name
