## Visual Direction
- **Colour palette:** Bold midnight navy (#0D1B2A) + electric blue (#1B98E0) + white (#FFFFFF) + subtle grey (#F0F4F8)
- **Typography:** Display: "Playfair Display" / Body: "Nunito Sans"
- **Tone:** Authoritative, professional, expert. This instructor isn't just good — he trains the trainers. Confident without being arrogant.
