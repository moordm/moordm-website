Our Story

The Digital Marketing Agency, Reimagined

Who We Are

Creating conversations with every click

Moor Digital Marketing was born from a simple observation: traditional marketing agencies don't evolve as fast as technology does. We started with over 10 years of combined marketing experience, but we realized the old playbook—manual campaigns, batch-and-blast emails, siloed systems—wasn't cutting it anymore.

So we reinvented ourselves. Today, we specialize in conversational AI and marketing automation. We work with small and medium-sized businesses to automate lead generation, intelligent follow-ups, and sales acceleration using intelligent conversation systems and automation workflows. Instead of creating campaigns that disappear into inboxes, we build systems that engage, convert, and grow.

Every conversation becomes an opportunity. Every interaction becomes data. And every business we work with gets to focus on what they do best while our AI-powered systems handle the rest.
